A Day in Nature

03/12/2022

Being in nature allowed me to get away from all the digital stimulus and distraction. I'm able to be more present and enjoy things that are happening right at the moment instead of constantly worry about the uncertain future. I was reminded, once again, that being with friends face-to-face is so much better than zoom calls! Nature is a place of healing, a magical force that sparks new ideas, and a start of incredible adventure!
